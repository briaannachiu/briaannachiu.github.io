Branding assignment for a fictional music festival. I settled on the name 'idio' taken from 'idiom' and 'idiosyncrasy', both terms which reflect the type of music festival that I was looking to create. This project was assigned in Introduction to Graphic Design during RISD's 2020 Wintersession.
