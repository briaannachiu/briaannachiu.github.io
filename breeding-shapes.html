Given a selection of specimens from RISD's Nature Lab, several images were chosen to serve as references for shapes that were then "bred" together. The first pool of bred shapes went through a selection process that involved voting on those that were most favorable. The selected shapes were then bred together to result in another pool of 24. Using the last pool, an alphabet was created through manipulating or reorienting existing shapes.
