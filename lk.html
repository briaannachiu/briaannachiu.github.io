I created a typeface specimen booklet for the typeface LK Better Days, which was created by Lettering Garage with whom I have no association with. The eight-page booklet is printed on thick drawing paper and bound with staples, the cover being a cutout backed with Dura-Lar. The booklet served as practice for typography, typeform, and layout. This project was assigned in Introduction to Graphic Design during RISD 2020 Wintersession.
