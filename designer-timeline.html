designed for desktop

The 2 Designer Timeline Project asks for a timeline presenting the careers of two contemporary designers. I was assigned Carin Goldberg and chose Leta Sobierajski as a second designer. I focused on the physicality of the timeline and the characteristics of each designer that I wanted to emphasize. Much of Goldberg's work is in print, which led to creating a booklet within the booklet in order to display her works accompanied by flaps which open downwards to reveal the description of the work and other events. Sobierajski's work is displayed with an emphasis on the bright and lively colors used in her work with work descriptions in the joined corners of each page, with additional moving parts that allow for the inclusion of more images.
