Four postcards illustrated in reminiscence of road trips and local vacations. For families unable to travel out of state or overseas, local sights such as gas stations and motels become new sights, despite their familiarity to locals. Each postcard uses a limited color palette and has one of two backings depicting modes of transportation such as a bus or train.
