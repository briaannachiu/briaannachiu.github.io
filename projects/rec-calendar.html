calendar design

The 2022 Recreation Calendar incites the user to take breaks for rest and enjoyment by providing possible monthly activities inspired by the season. The illustrations of such activities were modeled using Cinema 4D.
The calendar is printed on Strathmore Light Printmaking Paper and spiralbound.
