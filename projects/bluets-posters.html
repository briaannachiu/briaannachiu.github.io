poster design

Given the prompt to design ten book covers, 5 using photography and 5 using mixed media, the two of ten drafts for Maggie Nelson’s Bluets were refined. The two book covers then inspired two posters for Nelson’s book signings. Two book covers and two posters were finalized as a result for a reimagining of the novel’s cover and accompanying poster.
