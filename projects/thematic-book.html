"Ways of Communicating Ideas" is a thematic book discussing ancient written languages. The manuscript was provided by Franz Werner. This book approaches encyclopedic publications in a more modern and experimental way, in that it uses collaging as a visual expression. It strays from dividing textual information from visual information as such.
