Wish Maker

[Web Pages]
A Thing of Portraiture
Care Packaging

packaging design, pattern design

"Wish Maker" is an origami series utilizing patterns created using 5-6 swatch color palettes. The packaging is multi-colored with a patterned interior and a custom open-tab designed to prevent damage to the packaging and origami papers. Each package of origami papers includes several animal figures and Each package of star papers includes several paper stars.
"Wish Maker" packaging is printed on Strathmore Light Printmaking paper and assembled using Yes! Glue Paste. The origami and star papers were printed on Bee Sketchbook Paper using inkjet.
