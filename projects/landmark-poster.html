research-based design

The Landmark Poster is a project combining research and design. I was assigned Otl Aicher, the lead designer of the Munich Olympics' visual system and its pictograms. This poster is completely typeset in Univers in accordance to the values and characteristics which Aicher's team devised for the Olympics. I focused on incorporating dynamic movement and negative space while maintaining legibility.
